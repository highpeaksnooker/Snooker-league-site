League Rules

  1. Matches consist of 6 frames unless otherwise agreed by captains.
  2. Standard WPBSA snooker rules apply unless stated here.
  3. Start time is 7:30pm unless mutually agreed in advance.
  4. Phones must be silent during play.
  5. Captains submit results within 24 hours.
