League Rules

  1. Matches will consist of 6 frames, each played between two nominated players.
  2. Players must adhere to the official WPBSA snooker rules unless otherwise specified here.
  3. Matches are to start no later than 7:30pm unless agreed by both captains.
  4. Mobile phones must be on silent during play.
  5. Captains are responsible for submitting results within 24 hours of a match.
